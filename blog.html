Taqwa

When Umar ibn al-Khattab (رضي الله عنه) asked Ubayy ibn Ka'b (رضي الله عنه) about Taqwa, he replied:
"What would you do if you had to walk on a path full of thorns?"

'Umar said: I would gather my clothes, watch every step carefully, and move with caution to avoid being pricked.

Ubayy (رضي الله عنه) said: "That is Taqwa."
Taqwa is a state of the heart where a person fears displeasing Allah so much that they begin to avoid even the smallest sins, seeking His pleasure in every step—just like you protect your clothes from thorns.
