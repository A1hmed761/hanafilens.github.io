The Heart's Shield

Taqwa

تَقْوَى

Consciousness of Allah. The inner compass that guides every step away from what displeases Him.

Umar ibn al-Khattab رضي الله عنه asked Ubayy ibn Ka'b رضي الله عنه
Ubayy asked: "What would you do if you had to walk on a path full of thorns?"

Umar replied: "I would gather my clothes, watch every step carefully, and move with caution to avoid being pricked." Ubayy said: "That is Taqwa."

Taqwa is a state of the heart where a person fears displeasing Allah so deeply that they begin to avoid even the smallest sins — seeking His pleasure in every step, just as you protect your clothes from thorns.
